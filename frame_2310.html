Create New Slides with Text

Welcome to the lesson on adding text to PowerPoint presentations. Today, we'll walk you through the essential steps on how to add and format text, ensuring your slides are both informative and visually appealing.

You will learn how to use a text boxA tool for adding text anywhere on a slide to place content on your slidesA single page in your presentation, select a placeholderA pre-set content area on a slide layout where you can type text or insert media, and adjust font formattingOptions for changing the appearance of text, including font type, size, color, and style and paragraph alignmentThe horizontal positioning of text within a text box (left, center, right, or justified).
