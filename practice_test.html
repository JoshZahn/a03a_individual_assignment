Unit 3 Final Assignment: Create a PowerPoint Presentation

Activity Requirements

This assignment is designed to help you practice using all of the PowerPoint tools covered in Unit 3. Choose a topic — serious or fun — and create a PowerPoint presentation that meets the following requirements:

  • Your presentation must include 10 slides
  • Include at least 3 photos with labels
  • Include at least 1 chart
  • Apply 1 design theme consistently across all slides
  • Use at least 2 text colors effectively
  • Your presentation must have a cohesive look
  • Include a brief narrative in the "Click to add notes" section of each slide describing your experience creating that individual slide
  • Before submitting, review all slides for clarity, consistency, and errors

Grading Rubric (100 Points)

Your presentation will be evaluated using the following rubric. Use it as a self-evaluation checklist before submitting.

Criteria Excellent Proficient Needs Improvement Points
Topic & Overall Presentation Purpose Topic is clear, appropriate, and presentation stays focused throughout all slides. Topic is clear but some slides may lack focus. Topic is unclear or presentation lacks direction. /10
Slide Requirement (10 Slides) Presentation includes 10 well-developed slides. Presentation has 8–9 slides or slides lack detail. Presentation has fewer than 8 slides or incomplete content. /10
Images (3 Photos with Labels) Includes 3 relevant photos, each with clear labels that support the topic. Photos included but labels or relevance could improve. Photos missing, unclear, or labels missing. /10
Chart Inclusion Chart is included and clearly displays relevant data. Chart included but data or formatting could be improved. Chart missing or unclear. /10
Design Theme A design theme is applied consistently across all slides. Theme is applied but inconsistently used. Theme missing or slides visually inconsistent. /10
Text Colors (2 Colors Used Effectively) Two text colors are used effectively and consistently for readability and design. Two colors used but inconsistently or with limited effectiveness. Colors poorly chosen or not used as required. /10
Visual Cohesion & Organization Slides are visually cohesive, balanced, and easy to read. Slides are mostly organized but cohesion could improve. Slides appear cluttered or inconsistent. /10
Content Clarity & Review All slides have been reviewed for clarity, consistency, and errors. Content is polished and free of mistakes. Most content is clear but minor errors or inconsistencies remain. Content contains noticeable errors, unclear language, or has not been reviewed. /10
Speaker Notes / Narrative Each slide includes clear notes explaining the experience of creating the slide and using PowerPoint tools. Notes included but explanations are brief or incomplete. Notes missing or do not explain the process. /20
💡 Tip: Use the rubric above as a self-evaluation checklist before you submit. Make sure every requirement is met and every slide has speaker notes.
